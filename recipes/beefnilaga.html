Beef Nilaga Recipe

Beef Nilaga or Nilagang Baka is translated as "Boiled Beef". It's a simple dish that can be made by beginners as it's mostly just boiling the beef and adding your ingredients. It is best served when hot especially on cold and rainy days but it is still an enjoyable dish during a hot afternoon for its filling and delicious soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef brisket cubed
  • 1 big potato cubed
  • 1 bok choy or pechay
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 medium sized carrots chopped crosswise (~2 inch)
  • 1 tbsp whole pepper corn
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce patis

Instructions

  1. Saute the onion until texture becomes soft
  2. Add-in the beef brisket and cook for about 5 minutes
  3. Add the fish sauce and whole pepper corn then mix well
  4. Pour-in the water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 1 to 2 hours (or 30 minutes if using the pressure cooker)
  5. Put-in the vegetables starting with the potato and the carrots. Simmer for 5 to 7 minutes
  6. Add the bok choy (Pechay). Cover the pot and turn off the heat. Let it stay for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
