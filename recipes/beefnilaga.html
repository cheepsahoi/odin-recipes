Beef Nilaga Recipe

Beef Nilaga or Nilagang Baka is translated as "Boiled Beef". It's a simple dish that can be made by beginners as it's mostly just boiling the beef and adding your ingredients. It is best served when hot especially on cold and rainy days but it is still an enjoyable dish during a hot afternoon for its filling and delicious soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef brisket cubed
  • 1 big potato cubed
  • 1 bok choy or pechay
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 medium sized carrots chopped crosswise (~2 inch)
  • 1 tbsp whole pepper corn
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce patis
